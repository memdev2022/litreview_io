Welcome to the project

 Mapping Review on Memory Development

The RHYME team at the MPI for Human Development Berlin is developing an interactive version from the original publication Author & Author (Year). Title. https://doi.org/

All data and codes can be found at https://github.com/phucthuun

 WHAT YOU CAN DO:

  • Learning about the overall patterns in the experimental methods from the entire corpus under Overview.
  • Visualizing the methods in a specific task (or multiple tasks) with Hierarchical Edge Bundling under Explore.
  • Inquiring about inter-task similarity under Explore.
