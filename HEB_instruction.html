We offer three modes of the Hierarchical Edge Bundling

Click on:

  • Trend to visualize the combination of methodological variables for selected task entries. You can download the citations of the corresponding publications in APA-style.
  • Contrast to highlight methodological similarity between two task entries and their unique features. You can download descriptive data from this version.
  • Similarity to explore five more entries that share the most similarity to the selected task entries.
